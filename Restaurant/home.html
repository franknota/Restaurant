Frankie's Pizzeria first opened on July 7th, 1976 in Queens, New York City. We are one of the first pizzeria's in all of New York City that use a real brick oven. We are very proud of the way we make our pizza because of this old tradition that derives from our ancestors in Italy. Since our first opening, we opened multiple businesses in other areas including Brooklyn, Bronx, Manhattan, and parts of Northern New Jersey. Our pizza has been made the same way since we opened business. We are grateful that our business is expanding and always guarantee an enjoyable experience for every single one of our customers.
